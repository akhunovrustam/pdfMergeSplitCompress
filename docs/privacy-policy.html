Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2026

Introduction

PDF Toolkit ("the App") is developed by KIARVAS dev. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The App operates entirely on your device. All PDF processing (merging, splitting, compressing, encrypting, unlocking) happens locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Data Storage

The App only accesses files that you explicitly select. Processed files are saved to your device's Downloads folder. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any of your files or data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Storage access: Required to read PDF files you select and save processed files.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
